# 📖 Edirne Kırmızısı - User Manual

## Welcome to Edirne Kırmızısı!

This guide will help you navigate and use all features of the Edirne Kırmızısı e-commerce platform.

---

## 🏠 Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click the **"Giriş"** button in the navigation menu
2. Select **"Kayıt Ol"** (Register)
3. Fill in your information:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **"Kayıt Ol"** to create your account
5. You'll receive a welcome email

### Logging In

1. Click **"Giriş"** in the navigation menu
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click **"Giriş Yap"**
4. You'll be redirected to your account dashboard

---

## 🛍️ Shopping

### Browsing Products

1. Navigate to **"Ürünler"** (Products) in the menu
2. Browse all available products
3. Use filters to narrow down:
   - **Category** - Filter by product category
   - **Price Range** - Set min/max price
   - **In Stock** - Show only available items
4. Use the **Search Bar** to find specific products

### Viewing Product Details

1. Click on any product card
2. View:
   - Product images (swipe through gallery)
   - Full description
   - Price and stock availability
   - Customer reviews and ratings
   - Related products
3. Select quantity using +/- buttons
4. Click **"Sepete Ekle"** (Add to Cart)

### Product Reviews

**Reading Reviews:**
- Scroll down on product detail page
- See star ratings and written reviews
- View verified purchase badges
- Read admin responses

**Writing a Review:**
1. You must be logged in
2. Scroll to review section on product page
3. Click **"Değerlendirme Yaz"**
4. Select star rating (1-5)
5. Write your comment
6. Click **"Gönder"**
7. Review will be visible after admin approval

---

## 🛒 Shopping Cart

### Managing Your Cart

**Add Items:**
- Click **"Sepete Ekle"** on product page
- Item appears in cart icon (shows count)

**View Cart:**
- Click the cart icon in navigation
- Or navigate to **"Sepet"** page

**Update Quantities:**
- Use +/- buttons next to each item
- Click trash icon to remove item
- Cart total updates automatically

**Apply Coupon:**
1. Click **"İndirim Kodu Var mı?"**
2. Enter coupon code
3. Click **"Uygula"**
4. Discount appears in order summary

---

## 💳 Checkout Process

### Completing Your Order

1. **Review Cart** - Verify all items and quantities
2. **Click "Ödeme"** (Checkout)
3. **Enter Shipping Information:**
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Complete address
   - City and postal code
   - Order notes (optional)

4. **Select or Enter Address:**
   - Choose from saved addresses (if logged in)
   - Or enter new address

5. **Apply Coupon Code:**
   - Enter code if you have one
   - Discount will be applied automatically

6. **Review Order Summary:**
   - Check all details
   - Verify total amount

7. **Click "Siparişi Tamamla"**
8. **Confirmation:**
   - Order confirmation page appears
   - Confirmation email sent
   - Order number provided for tracking

## 📦 Order Management

### Viewing Your Orders

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"Siparişlerim"** in account menu
3. See all your orders with:
   - Order number
   - Date
   - Status
   - Total amount

### Order Statuses

- **🕐 PENDING** - Order received, awaiting payment
- **⚙️ PROCESSING** - Being prepared
- **📦 SHIPPED** - Sent for delivery
- **✅ DELIVERED** - Successfully delivered
- **❌ CANCELLED** - Order cancelled

### Tracking Orders

1. Go to **"Kargo Takibi"** (Shipping Tracking)
2. Enter your order number
3. View current status and location
4. See estimated delivery date

### Cancelling Orders

1. Go to **"Siparişlerim"**
2. Find the order (must be PENDING or PROCESSING)
3. Click **"İptal Et"** button
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund processed within 3-5 business days

### Reordering

1. View past order in **"Siparişlerim"**
2. Click **"Tekrar Sipariş Ver"**
3. All items added to cart
4. Proceed to checkout

## 👤 Account Management

### Profile Settings

**Accessing Profile:**
1. Click your username in top right
2. Select **"Profil Düzenle"**

**Updating Information:**
- Change full name
- Update phone number
- Modify email address
- Update password

**Changing Password:**
1. Go to profile settings
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password
4. Confirm new password
5. Click **"Güncelle"**

### Address Book

**Adding New Address:**
1. Go to **"Hesabım"** → **"Adreslerim"**
2. Click **"Yeni Adres Ekle"**
3. Fill in address details:
   - Address title (Home, Work, etc.)
   - Full name
   - Phone
   - Complete address
   - City, district, postal code
4. Check **"Varsayılan adres yap"** if preferred
5. Click **"Kaydet"**

**Managing Addresses:**
- Edit existing addresses
- Delete unused addresses
- Set default shipping address
- Multiple addresses supported

- Never share your password
- Log out on shared devices
- Update password regularly
